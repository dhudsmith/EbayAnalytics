The features of an ebay listing can affect the likelihood that an item sells. This bar graph examines the effects of various listing features (labelled on x-axis) on the likelihood of sale for 10,000 Apple MacBook Pro laptop computers. The y-axis is the portion of items that sold (Green) or didn't sell (Red) broken down by the item features. For example, nearly 75% of auction listings ended in a sale, compared to 55% for fixed-price listings. Similarly, the plot suggests that new items are more likely to sell than used items and that items with free shipping are slightly more likely to sell than items without.
