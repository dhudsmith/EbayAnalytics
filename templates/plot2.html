The features of an ebay listing can affect the likelihood that an item sells. This bar graph examines the effects of various listing features (labelled on x-axis) on the likelihood of sale for 10,000 Apple MacBook Pro laptop computers. The y-axis is the fraction of items having a specified feature for eaither sold or unsold items. For example, of those items that sold nearly half were 'Auction' listings, whereas, of those items that did not sell, only about a quarter were auction items. This suggests that the 'Auction' property increases the sale likelihood. There's also an inconclusive hint that 'Free shipping' increases sale likelihood.
